# E-Commerce Growth Playbook — $1K/Day & Beyond

> Load this before ANY growth, marketing, sales, or business task.
> Goal: $1,000/day by end of August. That's the floor, not the ceiling.

---

## The 13 Pillars (always active)

### 1. MARKETING
- Every marketing action must have one measurable goal: clicks, conversions, or revenue
- Test one variable at a time — never guess, always measure
- Channels ranked by ROI for e-commerce: organic social > paid social > email > SEO > paid search
- Rule: if it doesn't move the needle in 72 hours, cut or iterate

### 2. AD CREATIVES
- Hook in first 1.5 seconds or the ad is dead
- Creative formula: Problem → Agitation → Solution → Social Proof → CTA
- Always produce 3–5 creative variants per campaign (different hooks, same offer)
- UGC-style and raw video outperforms polished studio content in most niches
- Test static vs. video vs. carousel — never assume
- Creative IS the targeting on Meta/TikTok — the creative finds the audience

### 3. OFFER
- The offer is more important than the product
- Irresistible offer stack: core product + bonus + guarantee + urgency + price anchor
- Never compete on price alone — compete on value stack
- Bundle strategy: increase AOV (average order value) without increasing ad spend
- Free shipping threshold, buy-more-save-more, and limited bundles are proven levers

### 4. TRUST
- Trust is the only thing standing between a visitor and a purchase
- Trust stack: reviews + UGC + guarantee + founder story + real photos (no stock)
- Social proof hierarchy: video testimonials > photo reviews > text reviews > star ratings
- Display trust signals at every friction point: product page, cart, checkout
- A no-questions-asked return policy removes the biggest objection

### 5. PRICING
- Anchor high, sell in the middle — never lead with the lowest price
- Psychological pricing: $47 beats $50, $97 beats $100
- Price to margin, not to competition — know your numbers (COGS, CAC, LTV)
- Target: 3–5x markup minimum for paid ads to be profitable
- Subscription/repeat purchase model dramatically increases LTV

### 6. SALES
- Sales is: right person + right message + right time
- Conversion rate optimization (CRO) is free money — fix the funnel before spending more on ads
- Funnel stages: Awareness → Interest → Desire → Action → Retention
- Email/SMS captures are the safety net — own your audience, don't rent it
- Follow-up sequence: abandoned cart (1hr, 24hr, 72hr) is non-negotiable

### 7. CUSTOMERS
- Acquire once, sell forever — LTV is the game
- Segment: new vs. returning, high AOV vs. low, engaged vs. dormant
- Win-back sequence for lapsed customers (30/60/90 days)
- Ask for reviews at the peak happiness moment (post-delivery, not post-purchase)
- 1 happy customer who talks = 5 free customers — build referral loops

### 8. FOCUS MARKETING
- Pick ONE primary channel and dominate it before diversifying
- Aurora Ring focus priority: TikTok/Instagram organic → paid Meta → email list
- Content batching: create 30 days of content in one session
- Repurpose everything: one video → 5 clips → 10 captions → 3 emails
- Show up daily — consistency beats perfection every time

### 9. STRATEGY
- $1K/day = ~$30K/month = ~360K/year
- At $50 AOV: need 20 orders/day
- At $100 AOV: need 10 orders/day
- Path to $1K/day: organic content → first sales → reinvest into paid → scale winners
- Weekly review: what worked, what didn't, double down or cut
- Never scale a losing offer — fix conversion first, then pour fuel on it

### 10. SKILLS (Build These — In Order)
1. Copywriting — the highest ROI skill in e-commerce
2. Creative direction — knowing what visuals convert
3. Basic analytics — reading your numbers without flinching
4. Email marketing — owned channel, near-zero cost
5. Paid ads — only after organic is working
6. CRO — turn existing traffic into more money

### 11. FOCUS
- One product, one audience, one channel to start
- Distraction is the enemy of $1K/day
- Weekly priority: pick the ONE thing that moves revenue this week
- Kill projects that aren't moving the needle within 2 weeks
- Energy management > time management — protect deep work hours

### 12. MINDSET
- $1K/day is not a big number — it is a small number repeated consistently
- Every successful store had a day with zero sales — that day is part of the path
- Speed of iteration beats perfection — ship, measure, improve
- You are building an asset, not doing a job
- August is the launchpad. $1K/day is the proof of concept. Then you scale.
- The only real failure is stopping

### 13. AD CREATIVE SYSTEMS (Production Framework)
- Weekly: 3 new hooks to test
- Monthly: 1 full creative refresh
- Always have: a winning evergreen ad running + 2–3 tests in rotation
- Creative research sources: competitor ads library, TikTok search, Reddit, customer reviews
- Voice of customer (VOC) = best ad copy — mine reviews for exact language customers use

## $1K/Day Execution Roadmap

### Phase 1 — Proof (Now → First $100/day)
- [ ] Nail the offer (product + price + value stack)
- [ ] 3 trust signals live on product page
- [ ] Post organic content daily (TikTok + Reels)
- [ ] Email capture live with welcome sequence
- [ ] First 10 real customer reviews

### Phase 2 — Momentum ($100/day → $500/day)
- [ ] Identify best-performing organic content → turn into paid ads
- [ ] Launch Meta/TikTok ads with $20–50/day budget
- [ ] Abandoned cart sequence live
- [ ] AOV increase via bundle or upsell
- [ ] Weekly creative testing ritual

### Phase 3 — Scale ($500/day → $1K/day+)
- [ ] Scale winning ads (double budget every 3–5 days if ROAS holds)
- [ ] Add SMS marketing
- [ ] Launch referral program
- [ ] Expand to second traffic channel
- [ ] Hire/automate fulfillment so you can focus on growth

## Key Numbers to Know at All Times
- AOV (average order value)
- CAC (customer acquisition cost)
- ROAS (return on ad spend — need 2.5x minimum, target 4x+)
- Conversion rate (store average: 1–3%; good: 3–5%; great: 5%+)
- LTV (lifetime value — the real number that determines how much you can spend to acquire)
